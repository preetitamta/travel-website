Sach Pass Motorbike Tour

Sach Pass is a 4,420-metre-high mountain pass in Chamba District, Himachal Pradesh, on the Pir Panjal range of the Himalayas. It connects the Chamba valley with the Pangi valleys of Himachal Pradesh, India. There is one helipad on ascending towards Sach pass from Bairagarh of Himachal Pradesh.

The pass is open from June or early July to mid October. The road is narrow and unmetalled. It is the shortest and toughest route from Chamba to the Killar. Sach Pass bike tour takes one into a remote tribal region. It is “World’s Most Dangerous Road”. Riding on the edge of the narrow road with a straight drop of thousands of feet in the valley below. The biking tour to the valley is the best experience you can get.
