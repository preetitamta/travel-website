Manali to Leh Jeep Safari

Leh :-
Leh is a high-desert city in the Himalayas. It is a part of Jammu and Kashmir state. District Leh with and area of 45100 Sq. Kms. makes it one of the largest District in the country. It is at a distance of 419 Kms from Srinagar and 490 Kms from Manali. Leh is known for its Buddhist sites and nearby trekking. Every year Thousands of people from all over the World visit Leh by Manali-Leh road
Why Leh Ladakh?
Why to spend days on Jeep Safari for this tour?
Leh is one of the highest airport in the India.
The world's highest motarable road khardunga la.
Beautiful Lakes Like Pangong and Tsomoriri.
What else does one need?
There is fantastic scenery, historical trade route and breath-taking views. One who wants to enjoy it needs to be covered in 10 to 11 days. One can go directly to Leh by Flight but the better way is to do by road from Manali. And all days will come with the new surprises. Synopsis: Delhi - Manali - Rohtang Pass - Keylong - Sarchu - Taglang La - Khardung la - Nubra Valley - Pangong lake - Leh and Flight to Delhi
Duration: 11 Days
Best Season: June to October
